maintainer decision prompted by increment 38's probe finding (`f:add` couldn't consume a

  whole-number strings) to **long**, floating-point values (and decimal strings) to
  **double**. Result type decided over **all** args before folding (order-independent): any
  floating arg ⇒ the whole computation is double; all-integral ⇒ exact 64-bit long arithmetic

## Decisions
